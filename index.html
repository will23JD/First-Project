Welcome to Retro Bikes

Retro Bikes is a mountain biking group created by people who love mountain biking for people who love mountain biking. We are all about getting together, getting out on bikes and having fun. People of all skill levels are welcome to join whether a beginner or pro, Retro Bikes has something for everyone. So what are you waiting for get in contact now and join the crew.

Information

  • If there is anything you are unsure about drop us a message on any of our social platforms, which can be found at the bottom of the page.

Requirements And Safety

Retro Bike is all about enjoying time on your bike but things can go wrong some times so here are some things you must do to keep yourself safe on the trail.

  • Always wear a helmet
  • Appropiate mountain bike clothing. This will offer a lot more protection over normal clothes in the event of a crash and against the elements.
  • In the later months September - December it starts to get dark, so bring a mountain bike light (we recommend one for your handle bars and one for your helmet).
  • Everyone is at different skill levels, so if you don't feel comfortable with a section of trail then don't worry just get off and walk it. Or if you feel up to the challenge then just ask a more experienced rider for some help.
